To Micky Mouse

     

I’m honestly losing my mind over this. (oa) But since the year is ending, I want to start a new one with a clear and peace of mind. I’ll take the risk to tell you this.

     

I admire you, Micky. I like our weird stuffs together. I like it when you listen to me every time I talk. I like it when you’re just there being silly with me.

     

I’m so scared to let you know about this whole thing because I don’t want to ruin what we have. Kahit na hindi ko naman alam kung anong meron tayo basta alam ko na we’re happy together. We’re having a good time with each other and we’re comfortable na. I don’t wanna ruin such a good connection kasi I’m not sure naman kung anong nafefeel mo. I am not also telling you this to rush things or pressure you with anything. I just want to be clear with you so we won’t be living with confusion on New Year. But whatever your respond will be, please know that you’re the plot twist I didn’t expect to have this year.

        

Thank you for existing, Micky!
